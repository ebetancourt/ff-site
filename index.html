Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers. No jargon.

What kind of businesses do you work with?

We work across industries — from SaaS and fintech to manufacturing and professional services. If your business has processes that involve data, decisions, or repetitive workflows, there's almost certainly untapped potential for AI. We tailor every engagement to your specific industry context and operational reality.

How do you customize your approach for each client?

Every engagement starts with a deep-dive discovery session. We map your current operations, identify the highest-leverage opportunities for automation and AI, and build a strategy around your actual constraints — budget, team capacity, and timeline. No cookie-cutter frameworks.

What does "fractional" leadership actually mean?

Fractional means you get senior executive-level expertise — a Chief AI Officer or CTO — without the full-time salary and commitment. We integrate with your team on a flexible basis: it could be a few days a week, a specific project engagement, or ongoing strategic advisory. You get the leadership you need, scaled to what makes sense for your business.

How quickly can AI deliver real business results?

It depends on scope, but most clients see measurable impact within the first few weeks of implementation. We focus on quick wins first — automating high-volume, repetitive tasks — while building toward larger strategic transformations. The goal is always tangible ROI, not theoretical potential.
